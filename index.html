SUSTANTIVOS

As in English, los sustantivos are used to identify living beings, places, ideas and things.

En Español, we have 2 different classes of sustantivos:

SUSTANTIVOS PROPIOS SUSTANTIVOS COMUNES
Those that name places, cities and countries. They all start with capital letter. Those that make reference to animals, things and living beings. They all use lower case.
For example: Starbucks, New Jersey, Europa, Marie. For example: perro, casa, árbol, niño.

Depending on the quantity, Los sustantivos can be clasified in singulares or plurales.

Los sustantivos singulares make reference to an unique object.

Los sustantivos plurales make reference to 2 or more objects.

ADJETIVOS

Los adjetivos are used when describing sustantivos.

Qualities, colors, sizes, temperatures and many other qualifiers are therefore adjetivos.

ARTÍCULOS

Los artículos don’t have a meaning by themselves; they are just there to accompany los sustantivos.

Los artículos must agree in gender and number with los sustantivos
